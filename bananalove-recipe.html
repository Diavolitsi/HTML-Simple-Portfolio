Bananowelove recipe


Ingredients:

  • 400g natural yoghurt 1,5%
  • 10g cocoa
  • 20g WPC
  • 10g chia
  • 200g banana
  • 30g raisins

Instruction:

  1. Add to bowl: cocoa, chia, wpc and raisins.
  2. Pour yoghurt and mix it all together.
  3. Cut bananas into really small pieces or smash it.
  4. Mix it all together again.
  5. Enjoy.

Congratulation, you've made your first bananowelove!
